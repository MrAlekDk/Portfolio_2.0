Introduction

My name is Alexander Jens Peter Mavrina Sørensen and I am currently a student at KEA – Copenhagen School of Design and Technology, studying Computer Science (Datamatiker).

Growing up surrounded by family members being interested in music, technology and science, it only made sense that I eventually would follow their footsteps. However, before making the radical carrier-change towards IT and computer science, for the longest time I believed that I would be working in the field of science, either as a doctor or chemist, which led to my decision of studying pharmaceutical sciences at the university of Copenhagen.

Even from early childhood, I was interested in computers. I would use hours upon hours playing video games, drawing in paint or surf the internet. As I became older, my interested for tech and software only grew; How computers works and are build, how to edit videos and photos, how to record, mix and master music or how to live-stream to platforms such as Twitch or youtube. The list seems endless at this point. I always wanted, and still want, to have my hands in as many bowls as possible.
